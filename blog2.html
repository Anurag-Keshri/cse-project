Trending Technology is making us socially inactive

Have a look around, wherever you are, you would see yourself surrounded by technology. Everyday its a new model, a new gadget poping up in the market.

In these recent decades the use and development of technology has increased tremendously and everyday we seem to be wanting for more. Although technology has made our lifestyles easier, work efficient and less time consuming, it has also made us lazy and affected our health not only physically but mentally too. There are minor changes in our mood with the continuous use of social media which may later grow upto a serious condition like social anxiety, changes in sleeping pattern, stress etc.

We seem to socially connected on a large network with peoples around but it is actually a roadmap to social isolation. Now one can see a large number of people texting crazily on phone and less number of peoples meeting on a public park.

The major physical health issues include redding of eye, bad body posture, wrist pain, hand numbness and a lot more can be listed.

Evidently technology is beneficial to mankind but its negative impacts must not be covered under the curtain of positive outcomes.
